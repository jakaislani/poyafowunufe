Blac Chyna Accuses Tyga And Rob Kardashian Of Not Paying Child Support For Their Childrens

Blac Chyna recently announced on Twitter that she had to give up three of her automobiles due to a lack of assistance from her ex-boyfriends. The tweet stated:

“I had to give up three of my automobiles yesterday… My reasons are… principles, values, bringing a single mother, no support, and I’m a MAMA.”

In another tweet, she said that she was unmarried and did not pay child support. Despite her loss, she said that she was focusing on being grateful. A fan seemed to criticize the model by referring to Kardashian’s more visible efforts with Dream and writing:

“Is child support necessary when the other parent is actively involved in the kid’s life, taking the child on days and dressing and feeding the youngster?”

Single no support child support

— Blac Chyna (@BLACCHYNA) March 30, 2022

Chyna reacted with:

“No, it’s when the other parent’s parenting is effective; it’s called coparenting.” Greedy parents, on the other hand, strive to receive child support since they’re not doing anything [rocket ship emoji].”

After being accused of not paying child support, Tyga and Rob Kardashian hit out at their ex-fiancée, Chyna. According to Kardashian:

“I spend $37,000 every year for my daughter’s education.” I am in charge of all medical expenses. All of her extracurricular activities are paid for by me. From Tuesday to Saturday, I am responsible for my kid. “I’m not sure why I’d pay child support.”

Tyga’s response was as follows:

“I pay $40,000 a year for my son’s school, and he lives with me from Monday to Saturday.” “I’m not sure why I’d pay child support.”

A look into Blac Chyna’s personal life

In 2011, she began dating rapper Tyga, with whom she had a kid, King Cairo Stevenson, in 2012. They divorced in 2014 when Tyga started dating Kylie Jenner. Tyga and Jenner’s relationship sparked multiple social media feuds between Chyna and Jenner, which ended in 2016 when the former started dating Jenner’s half-brother Rob Kardashian. Dream Renee Kardashian, their daughter, was born in November 2016.

Rob Kardashian confirmed his divorce from Chyna in a December 2016 Instagram post. They reunited a few days later, but they divorced again in 2017. In 2019, Chyna and Kardashian reconciled their support feud. The latter had been paying Chyna $20,000 a month but had requested that his payments be reduced due to a change in his financial condition. His motion was granted when a court found that both Chyna and Kardashian should solely be liable for their daughter’s needs. Since the verdict, Chyna has not requested child support.

All about Blac Chyna

Angela Renée White, nicknamed Blac Chyna, rose to prominence as a stunt double for Nicki Minaj in Kanye West’s Monster music video. She rose to prominence after being mentioned in Drake’s Miss Me. In Encino, Los Angeles, the 33-year-old established her cosmetics line, Lashed by Blac Chyna, as well as a beauty parlor. Chyna previously launched 88fin, an online store stocked with new apparel and goods from her clothing brand of the same name. She has been on Keeping Up with the Kardashians and her own reality program, Rob & Chyna, with her ex-boyfriend, Rob Kardashian. Chyna has also starred in Zeus Network’s The Real Blac Chyna, VH1’s Love & Hip-Hop: Hollywood, and Tokyo Girls.
