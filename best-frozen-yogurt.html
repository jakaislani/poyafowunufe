Best Frozen Yogurt You Can Buy at the Grocery Store [Taste Test]

The best frozen yogurt is sweet, tangy, and creamy. And it’s available in the frozen aisle of a grocery store near you.

Up until now, I thought the best frozen yogurt was from a frozen yogurt shop. But I’m pleased to report that I’m wrong! You can buy great frozen yogurt at the grocery store. And not just plain frozen yogurt or even chocolate frozen yogurt—basically any flavor you could ever want. I had no idea of the world of store-bought frozen yogurt. Luckily, this taste test opened my eyes. I now know that frozen yogurt is the frozen dessert you should be buying at the grocery store because it is simply the best. If you’re looking for the best of the best, scroll down and check out the best frozen yogurt that even rivals the best ice cream.

Best Mix-Ins

Signature Select Low Fat Black Cherry Fudge Chip Frozen YogurtVons

Signature Select’s Low Fat Black Cherry Fudge Chip Frozen Yogurt has converted me into a cherry lover (a thing I didn’t think was possible). I’ve told, like, ten people I know to buy this since I tried it. It’s so cheap and so good. The cherry pieces are huge. There are tons of chunks of chocolate. The frozen yogurt itself is creamy. This tastes like something you’d get from a fancy ice cream shop for $5.75 a scoop. This is the best frozen yogurt for budget shoppers and flavor lovers alike.

Credit: Liv Averett / Vons

Rating:

9/10

Best Chocolate

Yasso Chocolate Fudge Greek Yogurt BarsTarget

For me, chocolate is king when it comes to desserts. Typically if it’s not chocolate, I’m not interested. These Yasso Chocolate Fudge Greek Yogurt Bars are an incredible chocolate dessert and definitely the best frozen yogurt bars. These are like richer and creamier Fudgesicles. They’re not too sweet and there’s no bitterness. Sometimes “healthy” products can skimp on portions. Thankfully, that’s not the case here. These are the perfect size. I will be filling my freezer with these when summer rolls around.

Credit: Liv Averett / Target

Rating:

9/10

Best Fruit

Noosa Strawberries & Cream Frozen Yoghurt GelatoTarget

Noosa’s Strawberries & Cream Frozen Yoghurt Gelato is almost as good as their Honey Vanilla Bean. Almost. That being said, it’s still a perfect 10 for me. Noosa knows how to make the best frozen yogurt. This has all the wonderful attributes of the flavor in our number one pick with the addition of pieces of strawberries. It’s also just a little tart. Perfection. Why are you even considering ice cream anymore? This is the best you could possibly do when it comes to frozen treats at home.

Credit: Liv Averett / Target

Rating:

9.5/10

Best of the Best

Noosa Honey Vanilla Bean Frozen Yoghurt GelatoTarget

I’m tossing every pint of ice cream in my freezer and stocking up on Noosa’s Honey Vanilla Bean Frozen Yoghurt Gelato. This isn’t just the best frozen yogurt, it is one of the best frozen desserts I have ever tasted. It has specks of fresh vanilla beans and an authentic honey taste. The yogurt base is creamy and not too tangy. It has the perfect level of sweetness. It’s flavorful enough to shine on its own but mild enough to compliment a slice of apple pie or fudge brownie. Go buy this!

Credit: Liv Averett / Target

Rating:

10/10
