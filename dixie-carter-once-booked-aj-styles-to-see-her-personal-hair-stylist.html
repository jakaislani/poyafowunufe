Dixie Carter Once Booked AJ Styles To See Her Personal Hair Stylist

AJ Styles is a workhorse and there’s no denying that. The Phenomenal One has put on classics throughout the years he’s been active in the industry. One of Styles’ recent feuds was with Grayson Waller, who insulted his “soccer mom hair.”

Styles brought up his hairstyle during a recent interview with Fightful Select. The 44-year-old recalled the time when former TNA chief Dixie Carter brought him into Nashville to see her personal hair stylist.

Styles confirmed a story that Dixie Carter once brought him into Nashville to see her personal hair stylist in the early TNA days, which is fantastic.

The former WWE champion also confirmed reports from 2015 that he was in talks with TNA about returning to the fold. He said Shinsuke Nakamura’s decision to leave New Japan prompted him to consider other options. Vince McMahon wasn’t sold on AJ Styles at first, but that soon changed.

Styles debuted at the 2016 Royal Rumble to roaring ovation. He feuded with the likes of Chris Jericho, Roman Reigns, John Cena, Jon Moxley (fka Dean Ambrose, and Brock Lesnar.
