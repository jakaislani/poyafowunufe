How Much Is The Net Worth Of The Office Starcast Paul Lieberstein?

Paul Lieberstein is the star cast of The Office as Toby Flenderson. He is also one of the writers and producers of the NBC sitcom The Office. 

He has been active in the entertainment industry since 1992. 

Early Life of Paul Lieberstein: Family And Education

Paul Lieberstein, aka Paul Bevan Liberstein, was born on 22 February 1976 in Westport, Connecticut, the United States. He was born to his parents: his father, Stanley Liberstein (lawyer), and his mother, Judith Liberstein (clinical psychologist). 

He grew up in his hometown with his sister Susanne Lieberstein and brother Warren Lieberstein. 

Regarding his education, Paul Lieberstein went to Staples High School. After graduating from high school, he attended Hamilton College and got his degree in Economics in 1989. Similarly, in 2011, he was recognized with an Honorary Doctorate of Fine Arts degree from Hamilton College. 

Soon after Paul got his bachelor's degree, he relocated to New York to pursue his career. 

Paul Lieberstein's Career Highlights: Known For His Work in The Office

With a major in Economics, Paul Lieberstein worked as an auditor at the Peat Marwick International for 6 months. Then, he went to work with his father in his law firm. Simultaneously, he started writing as a part-time job. 

In 1992, Lieberstein wrote an episode of the TV series Clarissa Explains It All. Later in 1997, he got to write 12 episodes of the TV show King of the Hill. 

Eventually, he also tried his hand at acting. He got his breakthrough role in 2005. He appeared in the NBC sitcom The Office as Toby Flenderson. Moreover, he also wrote around 26 episodes of The Office. Paul is also the producer of The Office, which premiered in 2005 and ended in 2013. 

The multi-talented 53 years old has 10 producing credits, 12 acting credits, 15 writing credits, and 4 directing credits as of January 2021. 

Some of his notable works are producing The Office, King of the Hill, The Newsroom, Song of Back and Neck, writing The Office: The Accountants, Space Force, and many others. 

Awards And Accolades

The Office cast Paul Lieberstein is recognized by several nominations under different categories. He has won around 11 awards, including two Primetime Emmy, one Daytime Emmy, two Gold Debry TV Awards, two Screen Actors Guild Awards, two WGA Awards, and Women's Image Network Awards. 

On the other hand, he has been nominated for 30 awards. 

How Much Is The Net Worth Of The Office Starcast Paul Lieberstein? 

As of early 2021, Paul Lieberstein is estimated to have a net worth of $14 million, according to several online sources. He primarily makes money from his diverse career as an American actor, producer, and screenwriter. 

Besides that, Lieberstein also makes money from his other business, social media sponsorship, and appearance in commercials. 

Personal Life

Paul Lieberstein is married twice. 

As of now, Lieberstein is married to Janine Serafin Poreba. They exchanged his marital vows on 19 July 2008 in an intimate wedding ceremony at the New York City restaurant, Battery Gardens. 

Paul and his second wife reside in Brentwood, Los Angeles, California. 

There is nothing known about his first wife.
