Apostle Suleman Sues YouTube And &amp; YouTubers Over A Video Made About Him Seeks $1 Billion

A document sighted online shows that Apostle Johnson Suleman has sued YouTube and some YouTubers over a video made about him that he regards as offending to him and his ministry.

Apostle Suleman through his lawyers has sued YouTube and 7 other YouTubers over a video they made about him which is offending to him and his ministry and is seeking $1 billion from them and also a video apologizing to him.

According to the document, the YouTubers are to do a new video apologizing to Apostle Suleman and retract or delete the old video they made about him and also publish a written apology in the 3 major national newspapers in Nigeria.
