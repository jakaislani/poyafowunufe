Neha Shetty Wiki, Biography, Age, Movies, Family, Images

Neha Shetty is an Indian actress and model who predominantly works in the Telugu film Industry. She made her debut with Mungaru Male 2, a Kannada movie directed by Shashank. She made her Tollywood debut with Mehbooba. She rose to prominence for her role as Radhika in the DJ Tillu movie. In 2023, she signed in to play the lead role in Bedurulanka 2012, Rules Ranjann, and Gangs of Godavari movies.
