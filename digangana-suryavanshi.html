Digangana Suryavanshi is a renowned Indian movie and television actress, author and singer. She is recognized for depicting the role of Veera Kaur Sampooran in the famous television series titled Ek Veer Ki Ardaas…Veera aired on Star Plus. In 2015, she also contributed in the TV reality show titled Bigg Boss season 9. Digangana was bestowed with Dadasaheb Phalke Award for the Best Emerging Talent for her influence to Bollywood cinema.

Digangana Suryavanshi can proficiently speak in English, currently resides in Mumbai. She also speaks fluently in Marathi and Hindi languages. Digangana Suryavanshi is the single child of her parents named Sarita Suryavanshi and Neeraj Suryavanshi. She mentions a lot about her maternal grandma and always dedicates her accomplishments to her as she considers her grandma is her biggest source of motivation as well as inspiration. Digangana Suryavanshi completed her school education from St. Xavier’s Secondary school, Mumbai keep score 76% in her 10th class board examination. She completed her intermediate from Mithibai College. Whilst she was shooting for the serial Ek Veer Ki Ardaas…Veera, she gave her 12th board examination and managed to score 77%. She is presently chasing her B.A. final year degree.

Suryavanshi initiated her career as a child artist when she was only 7 years old, initiating with the television series titled Kya Haadsa Kya Haqeeqat. She also portrayed supporting roles in the shows such as Krisshna Arjun, Ruk Jaana Nahin and Shakuntala. She landed her major break with Star Plus serial titled Ek Veer Ki Ardaas…Veera in the leading role, wherein she portrayed the title role. Her role in this series earned her a Star Parivaar Award under the nomination of Most Favorite Beti, besides conquering her Indian Telly Award nomination in the year 2014. Digangana Suryavanshi was one of participants in the television reality show titled Bigg Boss season 9 aired on Colors TV and was ejected on 7th December 7, 2015.  Suryavanshi also partook in sports reality show titled Box Cricket League Season one, played for Mumbai Tigers.

Digangana Suryavanshi made her first Bollywood debut with two movies titled FryDay opposite Govinda and Jalebi under Vishesh Films banner by Mahesh Bhatt and Mukesh Bhatt. Happening by chance, Digangana Suryavanshi had both the movie’s release date as 12th October 2018. Suryavanshi starred in the leading role alongside Govinda in the film titled Rangeela Raja released in the year 2019. Digangana also portrayed as a leading actress in Telugu movie titled Hippi, under the banner of V-Creations, as a female lead, produced by Krishnan K.T. Nagarajan.
