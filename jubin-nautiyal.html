Jubin Nautiyal is a most-talented person who is not just a singer, yet songwriter, performer, music composer and music director as well. He was born on 14th June 1989, hailing from Dehradun, India and he is the son of Ram Sharan Nautiyal and Neena Sharan Nautiyal. While his father is a renowned businessman and politician and his mother is also a business tycoon. The young, the emerging star has achieved so much fame and credit in his career life after doing a lot of struggle. He discovered his interest in music at a young age since he decided to join the music industry and pursue it as a career. Moreover, he is the victor of many awards and honors that he had won for his amazing contribution to the music industry.

Career

During his career life, he has sung many songs including various hits, also sung for Hindi movies, and recorded songs for movies in various different languages. He has established himself as a successful music composer and director, singer, and songwriter, as well. Moreover, he has won various awards for outstanding performances during his career life. He also honored with the award of the Best Male Vocalist of the 8th Year Mirchi Music Awards in the year 2016 for the song “Zindagi Kuch Toh Bata” from the superhit movie “Bajrangi Bhaijaan” starring Salman Khan. Alongside this, he gained huge popularity and recognition for giving the best to his fans and audience. In 2014, he dropped his first music single named “Ek Mulaqat” for the movie named “Sonali Cable”. Apart from singing, he is a well-trained music composer and is popularly known for playing harmonium, piano, drums, guitar, flute, etc. In 2014, he also dropped a song “Meherbani” for the movie “The Shawkeens”, another song is “Bandeya” from the movie named Jazbaa” released in 2015, and the most popular and most liked song is “Huma” from the movie “Ok Janu” (2016) starring Shraddha Kapoor and Aditya Roy Kapur.  Undoubtedly, he is blessed with a melodious voice and has won the millions of hearts of his fans. While his fans adored him so much for his wonderful singing skills that is enough to enthrall the audience. Now after constant efforts, he has become a leading singer in the Hindi cinema.

Achievements

Among various notable achievements, Jubin Nautiyal has won several prizes in form of awards and he was honored with the Musical Star Award in 2015 which he received at Zee Business Awards. Also, he was honored with the Mirchi Music Awards for the category of Male Vocalist of The Year 2015 for the song “Zindagi Toh Bata” for the movie named “Bajrangi Bhaijaan”.
